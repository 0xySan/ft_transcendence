About Transcendence 42

Transcendence is a modern web application where fun meets functionality, a social gaming platform designed to bring players together through timeless classics.

Challenge your friends or test your skills against others in Pong and Tetris, reimagined for the web with smooth real-time gameplay, dynamic leaderboards, and sleek design.

Built as a full-stack end-of-cursus project by ele-lean, etaquet, releroy, tgoumand and tle-goff, Transcendence 42 combines modern technologies with nostalgic gameplay to deliver an immersive and competitive online experience.

Play, connect, and transcend the ordinary.
